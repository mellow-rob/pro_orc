- On hover: background alpha transitions from 0.30 to 0.55 (matching real GlassCard)
- On hover: `Transform.scale(scale: 1.02)` wrapper with 200ms AnimatedContainer
- On hover: BoxShadow glow in `accentColor.withValues(alpha: 0.20)`, blurRadius 20, spreadRadius 2
- Resting state: no shadow
